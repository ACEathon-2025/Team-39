🧬 Cell Biology Summary

🔬 Core Concepts

Cell Theory: Foundation principle that all life is cellular

  • All living things are made of cells
  • Cells are the basic unit of life
  • All cells come from pre-existing cells

Cell Types: Prokaryotic (no nucleus) vs Eukaryotic (nucleus present)

⚡ Key Organelles

Mitochondria: Powerhouse of the cell

  • ATP synthesis through cellular respiration
  • Double membrane structure
  • Contains own DNA

Nucleus: Command center

  • Contains chromosomal DNA
  • Nuclear envelope with pores
  • Controls cell activities

Ribosomes: Protein synthesis

  • Free ribosomes: cytoplasmic proteins
  • Bound ribosomes: membrane proteins

Endoplasmic Reticulum: Transport system

  • Rough ER: protein modification and transport
  • Smooth ER: lipid synthesis and detoxification
  • Connects nucleus to cell membrane

  • Energy conversion is essential for all cellular processes
  • Genetic information flows: DNA → RNA → Protein
  • Cell specialization allows for complex multicellular organisms
  • Understanding cellular processes is fundamental to biology
