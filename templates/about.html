🚀 About OuchMyBrain.io

Born from our own struggles with traditional study methods, OuchMyBrain.io is an AI-powered learning platform designed to transform how students absorb and retain information. We combine cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive design to create personalized study experiences that actually work.

From AI-generated flashcards to podcast-style learning, intelligent study schedules to interactive professor modes – every feature is built with one goal: make learning more effective, engaging, and fun.
